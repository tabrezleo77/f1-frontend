This project titled F1 was developed by our team comprising of Md Kasif Ali , Mullick Waliullah, Aribah Tahseen and Syed Tabrez . We are students of Computer Science Engineering having a keen interest in f1 and wanted to build something that combined both our technical skills and our favourite sport. This is a basic data visualization project on each F1 players. Our main goal was to ensure upcoming F1 fans could gain knowledge through our Project and to test our skills hands on by developing a fun project. Basic Team Breakdown : Our two team members, Md Kasif Ali and Mullick Waliullah, handled the Front-end part of the project by using the tech stack html, CSS, NodeJS making sure the dashboard looked sleek and easy to read. 3rd & 4th team member, Syed Tabrez & Aribah Tahseen, handled the Back-end using Fastf1 by python . They managed data and made sure everything worked properly.
